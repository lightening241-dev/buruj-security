About Us

Protecting What
Matters Most

Buruj Security was founded in 1998 with a single mission: to provide world-class security services that give our clients genuine peace of mind.

Over more than two decades, we have grown into one of Australia's most trusted security companies — delivering comprehensive protection across every sector, from small businesses to large-scale events and corporate campuses.

Our name, Buruj — meaning "towers" or "fortresses" — reflects our commitment to building impenetrable layers of protection around everything our clients hold dear. Every guard we deploy, every patrol we run, and every system we install is backed by our founding promise of excellence.
